# Starting Charms & Merit-Flaw System

> Every soul arrives with something. The question is what it costs them.

## Core Idea

An optional alternative ruleset in which each player begins the game with a **Starting Charm** — a personal object tied to their soul's identity and reason for returning. Unlike Charms acquired during play, this one is chosen, named, and owned from the very start.

*See [[Card Types]] — Charms sub-type under Remnants.*

---

## Starting Charm Concept

Each player selects (or draws) a Starting Charm before the game begins. This Charm:

- Is **personalized** — the player names it and may describe what it is within the fiction (a ring, a photograph, a worn key)
- Provides a **passive bonus** or triggered effect tied to their soul's nature
- Cannot be destroyed by opponents unless a specific card effect says so
- Represents *why* this soul is fighting — their anchor to the living world

### Personalization Angle
Players could have a pool of Starting Charm options to choose from, or the Charm could be procedurally defined during a setup phase. The physical/video game version could let players write in a name and one-sentence description for the lore record.

---

## Merit-Flaw System

Starting Charms may come with **a bonus and a drawback** — a merit and a flaw baked into the same object. The thing that makes you strong is the same thing that makes you vulnerable.

### Examples (Placeholder — not finalized):

| Charm Name | Merit | Flaw |
|---|---|---|
| *A Mother's Locket* | Once per round, heal 1 when a friendly Soul enters the field | You cannot willingly sacrifice your own Souls |
| *The Unfinished Letter* | Draw 1 extra card during the Draw Phase | You must bid in every auction (cannot skip Acquisition) |
| *A Soldier's Medal* | Your Souls deal +1 damage on the turn they are summoned | You cannot re-summon Souls from the Liminal Zone |
| *A Broken Watch* | You always know the exact health totals of all players | Your mana hand cap is reduced by 1 |

> 📌 These are illustrative only — not approved card designs. Just showing the shape of the system.

## Alternative Ruleset Integration

This system is a candidate for the **Alternative Ruleset / Variant Module** framework first mentioned in [[Auction Initiator Variants]].

Possible implementation modes:
- **Draft:** Players are dealt 3 Starting Charm options and choose 1
- **Blind:** Each player draws a random Starting Charm from a dedicated mini-deck
- **Custom:** Players build their own Charm during setup from a template (name + choose one merit from list A + one flaw from list B)
