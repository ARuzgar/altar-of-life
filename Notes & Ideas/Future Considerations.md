# Future Considerations

Ideas and mechanics that are intentionally deferred — either too early to design, or waiting on playtesting data before committing.

---

## Valued Generic Mana
**Status:** Deferred — revisit after playtesting baseline

Assign numeric values (1, 2, 3) to generic mana tokens drawn from the blind deck, giving them variable power levels rather than flat flexibility. This would add another layer of variance and decision-making to the mana hand.

**Why deferred:** The charged generic system already adds complexity to generics. Stacking values on top risks cognitive overload early. Test charged + convertible first, then evaluate if valued adds enough without muddying the read.

*Related: [[Mana System]]*

## Health Overcharge Mechanic
**Status:** Planned — design TBD

Allow players to voluntarily sacrifice health during their action turn to power up an action beyond its normal limits. Possible implementations:
- Spend X health → gain X temporary mana this turn only
- Spend X health → draw X extra cards this turn
- Spend X health → bypass a card's typed mana requirement

**Design risk:** Must not trivialize the mana system or make health-drain feel mandatory rather than dramatic.

*Related: [[Health System]]*

## Health-Drain Cards & Summons
**Status:** Planned — card design phase

A category of powerful cards that cost health to activate, sustain, or summon — distinct from bidding cost. Examples:
- **Drain Summon** — a creature that deals heavy damage but drains its controller X health per round it's alive
- **Blood Spell** — a one-time effect that costs health to cast instead of (or in addition to) mana
- **Cursed Relic** — an equipment card with a strong passive that continuously drains the holder

*Related: [[Health System]], [[Design/Cards]]*

## Additional Mana Charge Types
**Status:** TBD during card design

The initial charge types (Pure, Cracked, Surge, Void) are a foundation. More charge types may emerge naturally from card design needs. Possible future types:
- **Echo** — when used, copy the effect of the last ability played
- **Tainted** — applies a debuff to the player using it
- **Blessed** — grants a small heal when consumed
