## Confirmed Design Decisions

### Core
- Health is the primary resource — soul energy, not hit points
- Mana is the activation resource — blind draws, capped per round
- Win condition: outlast all opponents (last soul standing)
- Players are **dead** — competing at the Altar for the right to resurrect
- Dying intentionally is valid and sometimes the point (epic loss, spite play, accepting fate)

### Decks
- **Three shared communal decks:** Mana Deck, Souls & Rites Deck, Relics Deck
- All players draw from the same pools

### Mana System
- **Typed mana** (school-specific, TBD names)
- **Charged generic mana** with riders: Pure, Cracked, Surge, Void (and more TBD)
- **Convertible:** 2–3 generics → 1 typed mana (exact ratio TBD via playtesting)
- **Valued generics** (numeric 1/2/3 values) — DEFERRED to post-playtesting

### Card Types
- **Souls** — field presence, spirits of the previously dead (auctioned)
- **Rites** — one-use invocations and spells (auctioned) | *Name: Rites vs Calls — both in play, TBD*
- **Remnants** — persistent personal/found objects (direct purchase)
  - **Relics** (sub-type) — found objects of power, impersonal, arcane
  - **Charms** (sub-type) — personal objects carried from life, intimate

### Soul Death States
- **Dead** → Soul is destroyed, sits in the Liminal Zone, can be re-summoned by paying mana
- **Forgotten** → Soul is permanently removed from the game (sent to Void/Purgatory/Chaos TBD)
- What causes Dead → Forgotten is TBD (specific card effects, dying twice, sacrifice, etc.)

### Terminology (Working — Not Final)
- Player elimination language: TBD — exploring "forgotten," "extinguished," "ceases," "silenced"
- Soul first death language: TBD — exploring "fades," "falls silent," "passes"
- Liminal Zone: working name for where Dead Souls wait before re-summon
- *See Notes & Ideas/Death and Forgetting — Terminology.md*

### Round Structure
- Draw Phase → Acquisition Phase → Action Phase
- Draw: discard unwanted, refill hand to 7, draw mana
- Acquisition: ascending clockwise auction (Souls/Rites) OR buy Remnant OR skip
- Action: play cards, attack, use abilities
- Health spent in Acquisition is lost before Action Phase — intentional

### Bidding
- Ascending clockwise auction
- Auction initiator advantage mechanic — ⚔️ TO ARGUE (see Notes & Ideas/Auction Initiator Variants.md)

### Tone & Aesthetic
- Melodramatic-romantic. Melancholic, not grimdark.
- Card aesthetic: ethereal modern tarot, constellation/foil line art, black ground, gold/holographic foil
- Physical cards: QR code linking to lore portal per card
- Inspirations confirmed: Persona 3, Death Parade, Tarot, various folklore/mythology
- Inspirations NOT yet approved for inclusion: Inscryption, Hades, Arkham Horror, others
